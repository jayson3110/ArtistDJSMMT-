H1GHR MUSIC의 디제이, DJ SMMT!


식케이(Sik-K), 그루비룸(GroovyRoom)과 함께 YELOWS MOB 크루의 멤버로 클럽신과 아티스트들의 공연무대까지 DJ계의 아이돌로 활발히 활동한 그는 최근, 프로듀서로 그 영역을 넓힌다고 한다.


DJ로 활동하며 많은 음악을 들은 것을 바탕으로 직접 비트를 만들며, 프로듀서로서의 작업을 시작한 DJ SMMT 이름으로 나올 작업물 들을 많은 이들이 기대하고 있다.


H1GHR MUSIC’s DJ, DJ SMMT!


Anyone who has watched a H1GHR MUSIC stage has once been captivated by DJ SMMT, another star who fills the stage together with other H1GHR MUSIC artists.


As a member of the YELOWS MOB crew along with Sik-K and GroovyRoom, DJ SMMT has been active in club scene and many artists’ performance stages.


He has recently stated that he will be expanding his work as a producer. Based on his extensive work as a DJ, DJ SMMT is making beats and many are looking forward to see his work as a producer.
